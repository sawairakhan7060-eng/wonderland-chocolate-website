Our Story

A quiet love affair with cocoa.

The Wonderland began the way most beautiful things do — late at night, in a small Lahore kitchen, with a spoon dipped in warm chocolate and the thought, someone has to taste this.

Today, we hand-bake every cake from single-origin cocoa, real cream, and the patience to let ganache rest overnight. Slow chocolate, in a fast city.
